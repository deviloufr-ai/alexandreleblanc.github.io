The Problem

Job searching without the right tools is a second job.

Beyond 20 active applications, standard tools don't scale — and the candidate pays the price.

01 / Fragmentation

Five channels, zero hub

LinkedIn, job boards, direct emails, networks, cold applications — no single view.

02 / Lost context

"Where was I with them?"

Impossible to find an application's history without reopening 10 emails.

03 / Hidden emails

Invisible ATS rejections

Confirmations, follow-ups and auto-rejections drown in the inbox.

04 / No priorities

What to do today?

No signal for who to follow up with, which interview to prep, what's dead.

05 / Rigid CV

Same CV everywhere

Sent as-is, no adaptation to job descriptions — hurts response rates.

06 / Wrong tools

Heavy CRM or broken Excel

CRMs are built for sales teams. Excel breaks past row 30.

Features

A single cockpit, powered by your emails.

Everything you need to manage an active search, without ever opening a spreadsheet.

Unified dashboard

All your applications, filtered by status, period or keyword. Stats and activity trends at a glance.

Auto Gmail sync

12 parallel requests on startup. AI reads your emails in batches and updates each application, zero manual entry.

Statuses & timeline

10 business statuses, auto-detect ATS rejections, dated timeline merged per application.

Next actions

Rule engine: overdue follow-ups, imminent interviews, prioritized urgent alerts.

Calendar & meetings

Google Calendar integration. Detect upcoming interviews, video call links one click away.

AI-powered follow-ups

Contextualized draft ready to send — never sent to a dead ATS mailbox.

Interview prep

3 STAR answers generated per application. Fast copy, ready for the big talk.

Adaptive CV

Upload PDF, AI rewrites per job description, export in a click — before/after side-by-side.

Under the hood

An AI co-pilot, never a data risk

All AI flows through a Vercel Serverless proxy — Claude API key never exposed client-side. AI proposes, you decide: nothing is written without your sign-off.
